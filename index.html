Your data is yours. We use it only to run KinetiQ and improve your experience in the app. We do not sell your data, we do not share it for advertising, and we never use it for anything unlawful. 1. What we collect The information you enter to use the app: your profile (such as goal, age, height, weight, and target weight), your food and workout logs, programs, daily check-ins (hunger, energy, soreness), body measurements, and progress history. We also store your account email for sign-in. 2. How we use it We use your data to provide the app's core features - tracking, progress, and your AI coach - and to improve the app experience. The AI coach is sent a summary of your own data so its suggestions are relevant to you. We use it for nothing else. 3. What we do NOT do We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers or data brokers. We do not use it for any illegal purpose, and we do not share it with third parties except the limited service providers below that are required to run the app. 4. Storage and providers Your data is synced to and stored with our backend provider (Supabase) so it is available across your devices and protected by per-user access controls. When you use the AI coach or AI food logging, the relevant text and a summary of your data are sent to our AI provider solely to generate your response. These providers process data on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes. 5. Your controls You can delete your account at any time from Settings - Data & Privacy. Deleting your account removes your data from this device; associated cloud data is removed as part of account deletion. 6. Children KinetiQ is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data. 7. Changes If this policy changes, we'll update the date above. Material changes will be highlighted in the app.
