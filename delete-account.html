Delete Your KinetiQ Account

We respect your privacy and give you control over your personal data. If you would like to permanently delete your KinetiQ account and associated data, you can submit an account deletion request using the button below.

Request Account Deletion

What happens after I submit a request?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted after your request is verified.
  • You will lose access to your workouts, nutrition logs, AI coaching history, progress, and other account data.
  • This action cannot be undone.

Data that will be deleted

  • Account information
  • Workout history
  • Nutrition logs
  • Weight and body measurements
  • Progress history
  • AI conversation history
  • Personal preferences

Data that may be retained

Certain information may be retained only when required by applicable law, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, security, or legal obligations. Any retained data is kept only for the minimum period required by law.

Processing Time:
Most deletion requests are completed within 7 business days.
